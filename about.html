Hello there! My name is Mavi Zabala and I am the creator of this website! As someone who has overcome the steep and tedious start in learning Japanese, I wanted to create this website to encourage new learners how to go and fulfill their goal of learning the Japanese Language! I started learning Japanese on and off at the beginning of my middleschool years-- specifically grade 8. Hopefully you enjoy/enjoyed your language lessons!

Nihongo to go is your friendly study website to kickstart your dreams of learning Japanese! This page will tackle basic greetings, Hiragana, Katakana, and some vocabulary words that you may use on a daily basis. When you're done with all that, you may also look my tips and tricks page for more information on how to learn japanese better!
